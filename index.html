🛡️ Important Disclosure & User Consent

ID Status collects and processes location data, motion sensor data, and media access to provide safety features such as crash detection and emergency alerts.

Location data may be collected in the background (even when the app is closed or not in use) to enable real-time safety monitoring and automatic emergency triggers.

By installing and using this app and granting permissions, you explicitly consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy explains how ID Status collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose
Precise Location To detect overspeeding and provide exact coordinates to emergency contacts during accidents or panic alerts.
Background Location To enable continuous safety monitoring even when the app is minimized, closed, or the screen is locked.
Motion Sensors Accelerometer and Gyroscope data are used to detect high G-force impacts (vehicle accidents).
Camera & Microphone Accessed only when the user explicitly initiates a safety recording or verification process.
Media Files To allow the uploading of profile photos or safety-related evidence (images/video).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Safety Alerts: Automatically notifying your emergency contacts if a crash or unsafe speed is detected, or if the "Panic Button" is activated.
  • Background Services: Maintaining a foreground service with a persistent notification to ensure stable monitoring.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing anonymized sensor data to improve collision detection accuracy.

3. Background Location Usage

Location is collected in the background solely to enable safety features. This data is transmitted to our secure servers only during an active safety event (like a crash) to alert your contacts. This feature is optional and can be managed in app settings.

4. Children’s Safety (Users Under 15)

Users under the age of 15 are protected by a restricted mode to ensure a safe environment:

  • No messages or requests from unknown/unverified users.
  • Inability to interact with public profiles.
  • Connections restricted to verified family members only.
  • Profiles are hidden from public discovery and search.

2. Identity Verification & Encryption

To verify family relationships and prevent unauthorized access, we use a secure verification process. We do not permanently store government ID numbers or raw documents on our servers. Verification data is handled with end-to-end encryption and is used strictly for one-time setup.

  • Encryption: All identity data is encrypted during the verification process.
  • Zero-Storage Policy: Once verification is complete, the sensitive ID details are purged; only the "Verified" status is retained to enable app features.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Sharing occurs only for:

  • Emergency Contacts: Sharing your location/status with contacts you have chosen.
  • Legal Requirements: Compliance with valid legal requests or safety regulations.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Users have the right to manage their data:

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated personal data via the Settings menu.
  • Data Purge: Upon account deletion, all location logs and personal identifiers are permanently removed from our databases.
  • Request via Email: You may also contact us at the email below for manual data deletion requests.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit and at rest.
