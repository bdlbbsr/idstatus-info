🛡️ Important Disclosure & User Consent

ID Status collects and processes location data, motion sensor data, and media access to provide safety features such as crash detection and emergency alerts.

Location data may be collected in the background, even when the app is minimized. However, location data is not collected after the app has been swiped away, fully closed, or after you have signed out. to enable real-time safety monitoring and automatic emergency triggers.

By installing and using this app and granting permissions, you explicitly consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy explains how ID Status ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Purpose
Precise Location To detect overspeeding and provide exact coordinates to emergency contacts during accidents or panic alerts.
Background Location To enable continuous safety monitoring even when the app is minimized, or the screen is locked.
Motion Sensors Accelerometer and Gyroscope data are used to detect high G-force impacts (vehicle accidents).
Camera & Microphone Accessed only when the user explicitly initiates a safety recording or verification process.
Media & Video Files To allow uploading of profile photos or safety-related video evidence. Uploaded videos may be processed and optimized securely on cloud servers to facilitate real-time streaming for emergency contacts.

Note on Sensor Processing: Motion data is collected locally using the device's built-in Accelerometer and Gyroscope APIs. Location details are obtained via global navigation satellite systems (GPS) and cellular network trilateration to ensure tracking precision during high-force disruptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Safety Alerts: Automatically notifying your emergency contacts if a crash or unsafe speed is detected, or if the "Panic Button" is activated.
  • Background Services: Maintaining a foreground service with a persistent notification to ensure stable monitoring.
  • Foreground Services: Maintaining an active foreground service with a persistent status bar notification to keep the safety monitoring engine alive and prevent the operating system from terminating real-time crash detection.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing anonymized sensor data to improve collision detection accuracy.

3. Background Location Usage

Location is collected in the background solely to enable safety features. This data is transmitted to our secure servers only during an active safety event (like a crash) to alert your contacts. This feature is optional and can be managed in app settings.

4. Children’s Safety (Users Under 15)

Users under the age of 15 are protected by a restricted mode to ensure a safe environment:

  • No messages or requests from unknown/unverified users.
  • Inability to interact with public profiles.
  • Connections restricted to verified family members only.
  • Profile information is hidden from public discovery and search.
  • Content is shared only within family members.

5. Identity Verification & Encryption

To verify family relationships and prevent unauthorized access, we use a secure verification process. All sensitive identity records or individual document verification data are strongly encrypted during transit and processing. Specific identification numbers are strictly encrypted and are not stored on our application servers.

  • Encryption: All identity data is protected via 256-bit secure encryption streams during the verification pipeline.
  • Server Protection Policy: No raw government identification numbers or sensitive verification source files are retained permanently on our central hosting infrastructure; only the authenticated "Verified" status flag is retained to safely unlock app features.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Sharing occurs only for:

  • Emergency Contacts: Sharing your location/status with contacts you have chosen.
  • Legal Requirements: Compliance with valid legal requests or safety regulations.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Users have the right to manage their data:

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated personal data via the Settings menu.
  • Data Purge: Upon account deletion, all location logs and personal identifiers are permanently removed from our databases.
  • Request via Email: You may also contact us at the email below for manual data deletion requests.

8. User Control & Privacy Choices

Users have control over how their information and app permissions are managed:

  • Permission Control: You can enable or disable location permission from your device settings.
  • Safety Features: Background location and safety monitoring features can be enable / disable from the app settings.
  • Profile Control: You can update your profile information and control what information is visible to others, where applicable.

Your continued use of ID Status after any updates means you accept the revised Privacy Policy.
