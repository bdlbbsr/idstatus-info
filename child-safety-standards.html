ID STATUS Child Safety Standards

ID STATUS is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe environment for all users. We do not tolerate child sexual abuse, exploitation, grooming, or child sexual abuse material.

Zero-tolerance policy Content or conduct involving child sexual abuse or exploitation is strictly prohibited on ID STATUS.

1. Scope and purpose

These standards describe the measures used by ID STATUS to prevent, identify, report, and respond to child sexual abuse and exploitation concerns.

These standards apply to all users, accounts, posts, comments, messages, profiles, groups, shared media, and other activity on the ID STATUS platform.

2. Prohibited content and conduct

ID STATUS strictly prohibits:

  • Child sexual abuse material, including real, computer-generated, altered, or illustrated material.
  • Sexual exploitation, abuse, or sexualization of children.
  • Grooming, coercion, manipulation, or solicitation of a minor for sexual purposes.
  • Requests for, distribution of, or promotion of sexual content involving minors.
  • Sextortion, trafficking, or threats involving children.
  • Attempts to arrange inappropriate sexual contact with a minor.
  • Content that encourages, normalizes, or facilitates child sexual abuse or exploitation.

3. User reporting

Users can report accounts, posts, comments, messages, or other activity that may involve child safety concerns through the reporting tools available in the ID STATUS app.

Users may also report urgent child safety concerns directly by contacting our designated child safety contact.

4. Review and enforcement

Reports involving possible child sexual abuse or exploitation are treated as high priority.

Depending on the nature of the issue, ID STATUS may:

  • Remove prohibited content.
  • Restrict, suspend, or permanently terminate accounts.
  • Preserve relevant account and activity records.
  • Escalate matters to appropriate law enforcement agencies or child protection authorities where required.
  • Cooperate with lawful investigations and valid legal requests.

5. Prevention practices

ID STATUS uses a combination of platform rules, user reporting, account enforcement, moderation review, and technical safeguards to reduce the risk of child sexual abuse and exploitation.

We continue to review and improve our safety processes as the platform develops.

6. Reporting to authorities

ID STATUS complies with applicable child safety laws and reporting obligations in the jurisdictions where it operates.

When required by law or when there is a credible risk to a child, ID STATUS may report relevant information to regional or national authorities and cooperate with authorized investigations.

7. Privacy and record handling

Information connected to child safety reports is handled carefully and may be retained where necessary to investigate the report, enforce platform rules, comply with legal obligations, or protect users.

Access to sensitive report information is limited to authorized personnel and service providers with a legitimate need to process it.

8. Designated child safety contact

Email: contact@idstatus.com
Website: https://idstatus.com
ID STATUS is committed to continuously improving its child safety protections, reporting tools, moderation procedures, and compliance practices.
